Landon's Hobby

I'm a man of many hobbies. Which one should I write about? I like to read, play guitar, sing karaoke, play with my daughter. But one hobby I've been involved in for several years is improv comedy.

My first exposure to improv comedy was going to a show with my family at The Second City in Chicago. I decided soon after to start taking classes there to learn the artform. From there, the rest is history.
